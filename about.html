About YouTube Thumbnail Downloader

youtube-tools provides a fast and free way to extract public thumbnail image links from YouTube video URLs.

What this site does

The website offers a simple tool that reads a YouTube video URL, extracts the video ID, and prepares the common public thumbnail image sizes in one place.

It is designed for people who want to quickly preview or download thumbnail images without extra steps.

How it works

The tool does not require sign-up, login, or account creation. It works directly in the browser and does not depend on a backend service.

It also does not use the YouTube API. Instead, it builds the public thumbnail image URLs from the video ID found in supported YouTube links.
