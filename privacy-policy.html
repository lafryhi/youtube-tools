Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains what youtube-tools currently collects, what it does not collect, and how future services may affect privacy.

No account required

youtube-tools does not ask users to create an account in order to use the thumbnail downloader.

YouTube links and storage

The site does not store the YouTube links entered by users in a database. The current tool works directly in the browser and does not rely on server-side storage for submitted video URLs.

No database in the current version

The current version of the site does not use a database to save user input, account details, or thumbnail requests.

Future analytics and advertising

In the future, the site may use analytics or advertising tools such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense to understand traffic patterns or support the project.

Cookies and third-party services

If advertising or analytics services are enabled later, third parties may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own privacy policies and service terms.

Policy updates

This privacy policy may be updated in the future as the website grows, adds services, or changes how it operates.
